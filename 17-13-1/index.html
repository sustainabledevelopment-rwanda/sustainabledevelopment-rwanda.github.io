Target name

Enhance global macroeconomic stability, including through policy coordination and policy coherence
Target number

Not available for this indicator
Global indicator description

This indicator provides information on the macroeconomic situation. It includes measures of: (1) Current account balance as a percentage of GDP; (2) Portfolio investment, net (Balance of Payments); (3) Foreign direct investment as a percentage of GDP; (4) Gross public sector debt, Central Government, as a percentage of GDP; (5) External debt stocks as a percentage of GDP; (6) Bank capital adequacy ratio; (7) Gross official reserves in months of next years imports (ratio); (8) Annual broad money growth; (9) Annual inflation, CPI period average percentage change; (10) Fiscal deficit (including grants) as a percentage of GDP; (11) Tax revenue as a percentage of GDP; (12) Annual growth of the general government final consumption expenditure; (13) Annual growth of households and NGOs final consumption expenditure; (14) Annual growth of the gross capital formation; (15) Annual growth of exports of goods and services; (16) Annual growth of imports of goods and services; (17) Annual GDP growth; (18) Annual average national currency per United States dollar; (19) Total trade in goods and services as a percentage of GDP
